Sum of Even Numbers in an Array

Problem Statement

Given an array of integers, calculate the sum of all the even numbers using an iterative approach.

Example 1

Input:
5
1 2 3 4 5

Output:
6

Explanation:
The even numbers are 2 and 4, and their sum is 2 + 4 = 6.

Example 2

Input:
4
10 11 12 13

Output:
22

Explanation:
The even numbers are 10 and 12, and their sum is 10 + 12 = 22.
