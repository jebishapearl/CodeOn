Sum of Squares of Elements in an Array

Problem Statement

Given an array of integers, calculate the sum of squares of all the elements using an iterative approach.

Example

Consider the array [1, 2, 3, 4]:

The sum of squares is: 1^2 + 2^2 + 3^2 + 4^2 = 1 + 4 + 9 + 16 = 30

Example 1

Input:
4
1 2 3 4

Output:
30

Explanation:
The squares of the elements are 1, 4, 9, and 16. Their sum is 1 + 4 + 9 + 16 = 30.

Example 2

Input:
3
5 6 7

Output:
110

Explanation:
The squares of the elements are 25, 36, and 49. Their sum is 25 + 36 + 49 = 110.
